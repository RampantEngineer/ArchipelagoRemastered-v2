Guide
BASICS
Drag Items Onto Others To See What You Can Create
IE: Drag Rock to Flint to create Sharp Rock
To Get You Started And Moving Around About
*Find The Items - 1 Vines/2 Branches
*Next Combine The 2 Branches To Create A Bundle Of Branches
*Now Combine The 2 Newly Created Items To Make A Raft

Important Tools
*Hoe
*Shovel(not Long Shovel)
These Tools Will Help You Survive From Starvation. On How To Make Them Is On Your Own.
*Tip : Dragging a hatchet over a combined item might split it back into 2 materials
